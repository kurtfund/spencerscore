Spencer Scoring & Stage is the connective tissue between world-class creative vision and the musicians who bring it to life. We specialise in premium orchestra contracting, music production, and global talent exchange — serving film, streaming, advertising, and live spectacle.

Led by Kirk Spencer, Director of Mercuri Holdings UK and a seasoned music producer and creative strategist — our work spans high-stakes partnerships with Live Nation and Apple to managing monumental symphonic performances with the Royal Philharmonic Concert Orchestra and RSNO.
